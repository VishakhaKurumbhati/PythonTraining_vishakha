GINI INDEX

Used to split data and it optimizes each split on maximizing purity(Homogeneous data)

4 RED balls &  0  BLUE balls-  this group is 100% pure based on colour
2 RED & 2 BLUE-      This group    100% impure
3 RED  & 1 BLUE -   This group is 75% pure (Homogeneous data)

Gini Score:
0 - It tells that about most pure group of data
0.5 - Least Pure data group
0.375 - Pure but not much.
